About Milaap

A dedicated platform to reconnect missing individuals with their families

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a safer, more connected society by using cutting-edge facial recognition technology to assist in finding and reporting missing family members or lost children.

Security & Privacy

We care deeply about data privacy. Only authorized individuals can view and manage profiles, and the facial data is encrypted and securely processed using mathematical representations.

How It Works

When a missing individual is registered, the platform trains a local machine learning classifier on face images. Users searching for a lost child can use their camera or upload an image to match against this trained database, quickly alerting authorities and parents when a match is found.
